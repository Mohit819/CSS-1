Paneer 65

Paneer 65 is a crispy dish with many spices.
Number Step
1 Put 20 paneer cubes in a bowl. Add 1 tsp chilli powder, ¼ tsp turmeric, ½ tsp garam masala, and ½ tsp salt.
2 Also add 3 tbsp curd, 1 tsp ginger garlic paste and 3 tbsp corn flour. Mix well.
3 Deep fry in hot oil, keeping the flame on medium.
4 Once the paneer turns golden brown and crunchy, drain off the paneer and keep it aside.
5 In a pan, heat 2 tbsp oil. Add a pinch of salt, 1 inch of ginger, 5 cloves of garlic, 2 chillies, and 2 dried red chillies. Saute well.
6 Also add 2 tbsp tomato sauce, ½ tsp chilli powder, ½ tsp salt, ½ tsp chaat masala and cook till the sauce turns glossy.
7 Now add a few curry leaves and fried paneer.
8 Mix well, making sure the sauce is coated well.
9 Enjoy!

Shrimp Tempura

Shrimp Tempura is a crispy dish and is very delicious.
Number Step
1 Wash and pat dry the shrimp. Make sure to peel and devein, however leaving the tails on.
2 Straighten the shrimp by cutting 6 small slits lengthways on the inside (make sure not to cut all the way through).
3 Turn the shrimp over and using your finger, gently but firmly press down the back of the shrimp all the way to the tail. The shrimps should be flat and longer in length.
4 Using scissors to trim the tips of the tails to clean them up.
5 Dip shrimps in a bowl of all purpose flour to make a light coating.
6 In a bowl, sift in the tempura flour and add ice cold water - refer to package instructions for the correct water to flour ratio. Lightly stir the batter with chopsticks (small lumps are ok).
7 Heat the oil in a pot over medium-high heat (check oil is ready by adding a touch of the tempura batter to the oil. It should rise to the top) Dip each shrimp into the batter and allow the excess to drip off then drop into the oil.
8 Cook a few shrimps at a time and work in batches for 2-3 minutes until lightly golden.
9 Remove from the pot and place on a plate with paper towels to remove excess oil.
10 Enjoy!

Chicken Wings

Chicken wings are juicy and deliciuos chicken parts
Number Step
1 Pat the wings dry with paper towels to remove any excess moisture. Place them in a bowl and combine them with 2 tablespoons of oil.
2 Lay the wings out on a wire rack in a single layer with space between each one, and arrange the rack on top of a baking sheet. Sprinkle the seasoning on both sides, making sure each piece is well-coated.
3 Pop the wings in the fridge uncovered for at least 30 minutes or up to 24 hours.
4 Set the oven to 450 degrees F with the rack in the upper-middle position.
5 Bake the wings for 20-25 minutes, flipping them halfway through or until they turn golden brown. Then, to make the skin even crispier, broil them for about 1 minute per side.
6 Allow the chicken to rest for 3 to 5 minutes before serving.
7 Enjoy!
