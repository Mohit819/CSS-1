Oreo Mug cake

Oreo mug cake are oreos mixed with milk that create a very nice dessert
Number Step
1 Mix the dry ingredients (flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder) directly in your mug or a small bowl.
2 Add the milk, oil, and vanilla and whisk until the batter is smooth.
3 Fold in the crushed Oreos and gently mix to distribute them.
4 Microwave for 1 to 1½ minutes at 800W, until the center looks set.
5 Enjoy!

Strawberry Shortcake

Strawberry shortcake is a dessert with juicy strawberries
Number Step
1 Place strawberries in a bowl; add sugar and stir to coat. Cover and refrigerate until sugar has dissolved, about 15 minutes.
2 Place 1 dessert cup in each serving bowl and smother with sweetened strawberries. Top each with whipped cream.
3 Enjoy!

Chocolate chip cookies

Chocolate chip cookies are sweet desserts that are chewy and are delicious
Number Step
1 Preheat oven to 350°F. Line baking sheets with parchment paper. In a bowl, stir together almond butter, chocolate chips, sugar, eggs, and salt until a dough forms.
2 Place 1-tablespoon mounds of dough 1 inch apart on prepared baking sheets.
3 Bake cookies until puffed and tops are set, about 10 minutes. Transfer to a wire rack; let cool.
4 Enjoy!
